Solution Method:
Use an online steganography tool to extract the hidden text from the image.

Recommended Tool: https://manytools.org/hacker-tools/steganography-encode-text-into-image/

Steps:
1. Navigate to the steganography tool
2. Upload the CSLogoCircle.png image
3. Use the "decode" or "extract" function
