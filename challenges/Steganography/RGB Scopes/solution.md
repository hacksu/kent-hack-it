Black and white images can be embedded within the bit planes of other images using LSB (Least Significant Bit) steganography techniques.

To reveal the hidden flag, analyze the individual bit planes of the image:

Tool: https://georgeom.net/StegOnline/upload

Solution:
1. Upload the image to StegOnline
2. Navigate to the bit plane analysis section
3. Examine bit plane B2 (Blue channel, 2nd bit)
4. The hidden flag will be revealed: khi{H1DDEN_1N_PLA1N_S1T3}
